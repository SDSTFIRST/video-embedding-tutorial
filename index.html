How to Embed a YouTube Video

Step 1: Find the YouTube Video

Go to YouTube and find the video you want to embed. Click the "Share" button under the video.

Step 2: Click Embed

From the share options, click on the "Embed" button.

Step 3: Copy the Embed Code

Copy the embed code provided in the box.

Step 4: Paste into Your HTML

Paste the copied code inside the <body> section of your HTML file. It should look like this:

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ly36kn0ug4k?si=D-xHgPowkUeJ8HFs"
title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0"
allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share"
referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>
